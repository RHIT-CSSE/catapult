House Painting

Experiment with Drawing

Spend a few minutes playing with Python graphics. (begin your code with from graphics import *)

You could keep enhancing your drawing forever, but we will stop at some point and continue our discussions.
But before we do that, I'll have everyone look at everyone else's drawing.

Getting started

  • Your task is to draw a creative rendition of a house that you design.
  • Look up the documentation for the Python graphics library (graphics.pdf); you can also find it in the Python Docs and Examples folder on your desktop). What other cool shapes could you add to your house?
  • Begin experimenting with some of these methods. Add a line or two of code and then execute your program. Once you get the hang of this, start drawing your house.

Challenges

  • Does your house have color?
  • Does it use different shapes?
  • Are there other parts of the scene beside just the house?
  • Can you use a loop to add an interesting feature to your drawing?
  • Can you make something in the picture move?
